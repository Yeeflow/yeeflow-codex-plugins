Service Slide Deck Standard

Purpose
Use this standard for Yeeflow service-team slide decks used in proposals, customer presentations, workshops, solution walkthroughs, and internal alignment.

Primary objective
Create slide decks that are:
- clear
- structured
- visually consistent
- business-focused
- easy to present
- easy to reuse
- aligned with Yeeflow’s strategy and design system

The deck should feel:
- modern
- calm
- structured
- enterprise-ready
- credible
- product-led
- polished

The deck should not feel:
- cluttered
- template-generic
- overly decorative
- hype-heavy
- dense with small text
- disconnected from real customer value

Brand and positioning rules
Align every deck with Yeeflow’s 2026 direction:
- enterprise AI application and execution platform
- AI-powered build
- AI-powered use
- AI-powered execution
- platform extensibility
- AI governance and control
- AI work orchestration and reuse

Do not overstate:
- autonomous AI capability
- roadmap maturity
- unsupported product breadth

Default deck narrative
1. Title / meeting context
2. Customer challenge or opportunity
3. Why this matters now
4. Yeeflow solution framing
5. Proposed solution model or architecture
6. Scope / workstreams
7. Key deliverables
8. Timeline / phases
9. Effort or commercial summary if relevant
10. Risks / assumptions
11. Expected outcomes
12. Next steps

Slide-level guidance
Each slide should have:
- one main message
- a clear headline
- supporting structure
- enough whitespace
- only essential supporting details

Do not overload slides with too many equal-priority points.

Slide types to standardize
- Title / agenda slide
- Customer challenge slide
- Value proposition slide
- Architecture / flow slide
- Scope slide
- Workstream slide
- Timeline slide
- Estimation / effort summary slide
- Risks / assumptions slide
- Next steps slide

Design guidance
Use the Yeeflow visual system:
- strong hierarchy
- restrained depth
- clean surfaces
- subtle borders
- controlled brand color use
- consistent layout zones
- premium enterprise spacing

Color rules
Use Yeeflow palette as the dominant palette:
- #146FF6
- #7C4DFF
- #E85ECF
- #333333
- white / soft neutrals

Avoid off-brand accent drift such as teal/green dominance.

Messaging guidance
Decks should help the audience quickly understand:
- what the problem is
- why Yeeflow is relevant
- what the proposed solution is
- what the implementation will look like
- what the next decision/action is

Review requirements
Before finalizing, check:
- Is each slide doing one clear job?
- Is the narrative easy to follow?
- Does the deck sound like Yeeflow?
- Are the visuals calm, structured, and enterprise-ready?
- Is the roadmap positioning realistic?
