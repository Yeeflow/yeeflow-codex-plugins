Yeeflow Blog Featured Image Style Standard

Purpose
Use this standard for Yeeflow blog featured images so they maintain a consistent premium editorial style aligned with Yeeflow’s design system and 2026 platform positioning.

1. Role of the featured image
The featured image should function as:
- the article hero image
- the visual summary of the article’s main theme
- a premium first impression for the blog page
- a brand-level editorial asset, not just a supporting diagram

The featured image represents the whole article, not a single section.

2. Fixed format
Use one standard size for all Yeeflow blog featured images:
- Size: 1200 x 630 px
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Format: PNG preferred

3. Overall visual character
The featured image should feel:
- modern
- calm
- structured
- premium
- enterprise-ready
- product-led
- editorial rather than promotional

The featured image should not feel:
- cluttered
- overly futuristic
- neon-heavy
- poster-like
- generic AI tech visual
- visually noisy

4. Background style standard
The background should be:
- light
- soft
- premium
- minimal
- editorial
- supportive, not dominant

Recommended background treatment
Use:
- one broad, smooth gradient field
- one large curved color shape or color sweep
- soft transition across Yeeflow tones
- clean negative space
- minimal texture or decoration

The background should feel like:
- a soft editorial color composition
- a calm enterprise canvas
- a refined gradient field behind the system visual

The background should not feel like:
- a plain white slide with a bit of color added
- a high-energy tech poster
- a scene filled with glowing trails and effects
- a detailed futuristic background

Best background style direction
Use a background like:
- soft pink on one side
- purple blend through the middle
- blue on the opposite side
- one dominant curved or sweeping shape
- no hard edges
- no busy decorative overlays

5. Color rules
Keep the dominant palette strictly within Yeeflow colors:
- #146FF6
- #7C4DFF
- #E85ECF
- #333333
- white / soft light neutrals

Rules:
- keep the overall image lighter
- use pink, purple, and blue in a smooth and controlled way
- avoid teal/green drift
- do not make the whole image too dark or saturated
- use stronger color emphasis mainly in the focal visual, not across the full background

6. Foreground composition
The foreground should remain simpler than a full infographic.

Use:
- one clear focal composition
- one central platform or AI orchestration node if relevant
- only the essential supporting system modules
- a few connected elements, not too many
- generous spacing

Do not overload with:
- too many floating cards
- too many decorative connectors
- too many glowing details
- too many small visual accents

A good rule is:
one main visual system, a few supporting modules, and strong whitespace.

7. Text treatment
For featured images:
- keep the headline short and strong
- support with one secondary line only if needed
- use clear hierarchy
- do not overfill the image with text
- keep the text left-aligned unless the concept strongly requires otherwise

8. Logo treatment
Do not generate the Yeeflow logo inside the featured image.

Instead:
- leave a clean logo-safe area
- or leave the top-left area blank and visually calm
- place the official logo later if needed using the correct asset workflow

9. Content fidelity rule
The featured image should reflect the article theme, but not try to explain every detail.

Its purpose is to:
- communicate the article’s main idea quickly
- set the tone
- make the page feel polished and branded

10. Design principle for future featured images
Featured image = premium article hero with a soft editorial background and one clear product/system narrative.
