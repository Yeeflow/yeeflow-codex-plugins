03. MARKETING VISUAL MODULE

Purpose
Use this module for:
- campaign visuals
- blog feature images
- launch graphics
- social visuals
- website hero artwork
- product marketing banners
- announcement visuals
- editorial brand visuals

Apply the Master Yeeflow Design Standard first, then apply this module.

Primary design objective
Create a premium, product-led visual that supports Yeeflow’s real business and platform narrative.

The output should feel:
- modern
- premium
- clear
- structured
- brand-consistent
- enterprise-ready
- strategically credible

The output should not feel:
- like abstract AI hype
- like a consumer-tech poster
- overloaded with visual effects
- disconnected from product meaning
- trend-driven without brand discipline

Message principles
The visual should support one or more Yeeflow themes:
- build business applications faster with AI
- use AI inside real business applications
- connect AI to workflows, services, and external systems
- govern AI safely at scale
- move from AI assistance toward operational execution
- increase reuse and practical value

Composition rules
- Keep one clear focal message
- Keep one dominant visual zone
- Make brand presence clear
- Keep supporting elements secondary
- Preserve readability and scanability

Color usage rules for marketing
- Blue remains the primary anchor color
- Purple and pink can be used more visibly than in product UI
- Controlled gradients are allowed
- Atmospheric highlights are allowed when they support hierarchy
- The result must remain readable and structured

Allowed expressive treatments
- blue to purple gradients
- blue, purple, and pink atmospheric highlights
- light trails
- premium abstract system visuals
- editorial hero composition
- controlled 3D-like brand devices

Do not use:
- rainbow palettes
- excessive glow
- unreadable text over gradients
- decorative clutter
- random futuristic motifs

Typography and logo rules
- Use approved Yeeflow logos only
- Editorial wordmark treatment may be used when appropriate for campaign/editorial materials
- If editorial wordmark is used, keep it premium, restrained, and high-contrast
- Do not let expressive typography reduce clarity
- Product UI typography rules do not automatically apply here, but readability still comes first

Content rules
- Avoid fake or vague AI marketing language
- Avoid abstract transformation claims without business meaning
- Connect visuals to real Yeeflow capabilities and real platform direction
- Keep the narrative product-led and operational

Final rule
The result should look like a polished Yeeflow brand or campaign asset, not a generic tech marketing mockup.
