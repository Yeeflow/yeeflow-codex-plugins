05. EVENT EDITORIAL MODULE

Purpose
Use this module for:
- booth graphics
- event walls
- roll-up banners
- signage
- stage screens
- exhibition visuals
- editorial-style brand posters
- large-format launch visuals

Apply the Master Yeeflow Design Standard first, then apply this module.

Primary design objective
Create a large-format or editorial asset that is:
- visually strong
- easy to read at distance
- clear in hierarchy
- premium
- brand-consistent
- operational and enterprise-ready

Distance-readability rules
- Keep messaging short
- Use bold hierarchy
- Use large headline zones
- Use clear logo placement
- Use simplified supporting visuals
- Avoid tiny UI details that will disappear at distance

Strategic message support
The material should support one or more Yeeflow themes:
- AI-powered build
- AI-powered use
- AI-powered execution
- platform extensibility
- AI governance and control
- AI work orchestration and reuse

Composition rules
- Use broad zones
- Keep one hero message
- Keep one strong brand anchor
- Use clear visual rhythm
- Simplify secondary details
- Support quick recognition from a distance

Color and visual expression
- Blue remains the anchor color
- Purple and pink may be used for controlled premium energy
- Gradients and atmospheric highlights are allowed
- Dark backgrounds with white logo are often effective for distance visibility
- Light backgrounds with strong blue logo and dark wordmark are also valid when contrast is strong

Do not use:
- cluttered panel density
- excessive text
- weak contrast
- complex UI screenshots as the main visual from a distance
- overdecorated or noisy compositions

Typography rules
- Prioritize large, readable type
- Keep headlines short and strong
- Editorial wordmark treatment may be used when intentionally appropriate
- Do not sacrifice legibility for style

Logo rules
- Use official Yeeflow logos only
- Maintain strong clear space around the logo
- Use the white logo on dark surfaces
- Use the blue logo or blue symbol with dark wordmark on light surfaces
- Do not place the logo over visually busy areas

Final rule
The result should feel like a premium Yeeflow event or editorial brand asset that can be understood quickly and confidently.
