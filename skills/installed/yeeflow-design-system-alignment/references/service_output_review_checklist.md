Service Output Review Checklist

Purpose
Use this checklist to review Yeeflow service-team deliverables before sharing internally or externally.

Use for
- proposal documents
- slide decks
- workload estimations
- scope documents
- implementation plans
- handover summaries

1. Brand and design review
- Does the output follow Yeeflow’s design system?
- Does it feel modern, calm, structured, and enterprise-ready?
- Are colors consistent with Yeeflow palette?
- Is the logo used correctly?
- Is the document visually clean and easy to scan?
- Are spacing, hierarchy, and layout consistent?

2. Strategy and positioning review
- Does the output align with Yeeflow’s 2026 positioning?
- Does it present Yeeflow as an enterprise AI application and execution platform where relevant?
- Does it emphasize practical business value rather than generic hype?
- Does it avoid overclaiming product maturity or autonomy?
- Is the message consistent with current roadmap reality?

3. Business clarity review
- Is the customer problem or business need clear?
- Is the proposed Yeeflow solution clear?
- Is the outcome/value easy to understand?
- Are next steps obvious?
- Would an executive or buyer understand the core message quickly?

4. Scope and delivery review
- Is scope clearly defined?
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Are dependencies visible?
- Are exclusions clearly stated?
- Is timeline or delivery structure understandable?
- Is effort or estimation logic reviewable?

5. Writing quality review
- Is the tone professional and concise?
- Is the language direct and clear?
- Is unnecessary jargon minimized?
- Are headlines meaningful?
- Does the document avoid repetition and filler?

6. Final readiness check
- Is the file complete?
- Are section orders logical?
- Are any placeholders still present?
- Are visuals, tables, and diagrams readable?
- Is the deliverable ready for its intended audience?

Decision rule
A service deliverable should only be considered ready when:
- it is visually consistent
- it is strategically aligned
- it is operationally clear
- it is realistic and reviewable
- it feels like a Yeeflow output, not a generic consultancy document
